# [Exponentiation Operator](https://github.com/rwaldron/exponentiation-operator)

## BinaryOperator

```js
extend enum BinaryOperator {
    "==" | "!=" | "===" | "!=="
         | "<" | "<=" | ">" | ">="
         | "<<" | ">>" | ">>>"
         | "+" | "-" | "*" | "**" | "/" | "%"
         | "|" | "^" | "&" | "in"
         | "instanceof"
}
```

## AssignmentOperator

```js
extend enum AssignmentOperator {
    "=" | "+=" | "-=" | "*=" | "**=" | "/=" | "%="
        | "<<=" | ">>=" | ">>>="
        | "|=" | "^=" | "&="
}
```
